Mobile application: MentorMate

Link to GitHub

Presentation of the project

The idea of the application is to connect students who want to do well in their dream job with experienced experts in that field. This app will provide personalized long-term guidance, the best resources, and specific advice for the job they want. There's also a unique opportunity: mentors can recommend students after the mentoring period, helping them smoothly transition into the professional world.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: Flutter
  • Backend: Firebase
  • APIs: Kommunicate API for the chatbot, OpenWeather API for weather data, Custom API for password reset (Forget Password)

Functionalities

The principal page features a "Get Started" button, guiding users to a subsequent page where they can choose to connect either as a mentor or as a student.

Both mentors and students use the same page to enter their accounts. But if you don't have an account yet, mentors and students each have their own page to create a new account.

For the student:

As a student in the app, you can access the navigation bar, where you'll find options such as Menu, Home, Our Mentors,Our offers, Projects, About Us, and Contact.

For the mentor:

As a mentor in the app, you can access the navigation bar, where you'll find options such as Menu, Home,Our students, About Us, and Contact.
