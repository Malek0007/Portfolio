ABOUT Me

AI enthusiast

I am a final-year engineering student at the Higher School of Communication in Tunis, with a strong passion for AI and Generative AI. My interest in AI began during my preparatory school years when I attended a conference titled "The Power of AI." That experience sparked my curiosity and inspired me to explore this fascinating field.

Throughout my two years in preparatory school, I developed strong skills in mathematics, including calculus and linear algebra, and gained a solid foundation in computer science by mastering Python. I successfully passed the national competitive examinations for admission to engineering training cycles, ranking 44 out of 1400, and was selected to attend one of the top engineering universities in Tunisia.

My journey at the Higher School of Communication has further deepened my interest in AI. I have explored various domains, including machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing, through coursework and labs. I have also participated in numerous events, winning hackathons that provided me with valuable experience in developing innovative solutions within the AI domain.

In addition, I have gained essential soft skills such as leadership, by leading multiple projects in hackathons, clubs and academic projects, as well as effective team collaboration, adaptability, and problem-solving. These skills have enabled me to manage complex tasks, work well under pressure, and communicate efficiently within diverse teams.

I am proud of the projects I have completed, such as the text generation application, user dashboard with chatbot, Cloud Native e-commerce application with DevOps automation, my chatbot, text summarizer, and plant disease detection system. These experiences have fueled my thirst for knowledge and my desire to expand my skills. I am highly motivated to take on more projects and continue pushing the boundaries of what I can achieve in this exciting field.
